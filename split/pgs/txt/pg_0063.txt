approach, virtually any computationally tractable structured model can be used as a role labeler, including almost any semantic role labeler introduced in the context of supervised SRL (see, e.g., CoNLL shared tasks (Carreras and M` arquez, 2005; Surdeanu et al., 2008; Haji c et al., 2009)). This opens interesting possibilities to extend our approach to the semi-supervised setting. Previous unsupervised SRL models make too strong assumption and use too limited features to effectively exploit labeled data. For our model, the reconstruction objective can be easily combined with the likelihood objective, yielding a potentially powerful semi-supervised method. We leave this direction for future work.
